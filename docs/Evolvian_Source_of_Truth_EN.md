# Evolvian — Product Guide & FAQ
Last updated: March 2026

## What is Evolvian?

Evolvian is an AI assistant platform built for businesses. It lets you deploy a smart AI assistant trained on your own business content — so your customers get instant, accurate answers 24/7 without your team having to respond manually every time.

In simple terms: **Evolvian turns your business knowledge into always-on AI support.**

Your assistant can:
- Answer customer questions instantly, day or night
- Handle appointment booking and send reminders automatically
- Capture leads and contact details
- Work across your website chat, WhatsApp, and email
- Keep your team free from repetitive, time-consuming conversations

---

## Who is Evolvian for?

Evolvian is built for teams that need real automation — not just a basic chatbot.

It's a great fit if you are:
- A small or medium business that receives frequent customer messages and wants to respond faster
- A service business (clinic, salon, consultant) that books appointments and wants to reduce no-shows
- A sales or support team that wants consistent answers at scale without hiring more staff
- An operations team that needs a reliable, always-available assistant across multiple channels

Whether you handle 100 or 5,000 customer conversations a month, Evolvian grows with you.

## What can Evolvian do?

### AI assistant trained on your business content
Upload your own documents (PDF or TXT files) — like your FAQs, service descriptions, pricing guides, or policies — and Evolvian's assistant learns from them. When customers ask questions, the assistant answers using your actual business information, not generic responses.

### Website chat widget
Add a chat widget to your website in minutes. Customers can open a chat window and get answers immediately. You can configure the assistant's name, appearance, and behavior from your dashboard.

### WhatsApp integration
Connect your WhatsApp Business account and let Evolvian handle incoming messages automatically. Your assistant answers customer questions, books appointments, and sends confirmations — directly through WhatsApp.

### Email workflows
Enable email-based support and follow-ups. Evolvian can send appointment confirmations and reminders via email. Available on eligible plans.

### Appointment booking and reminders
Evolvian can handle your scheduling: it shows available time slots, collects customer information, books the appointment, and sends automatic reminders to reduce no-shows. No manual coordination needed.

### Google Calendar sync
Connect your Google Calendar and Evolvian will only offer time slots that are actually available. It avoids double-booking and respects your booking rules (advance notice required, buffer time between appointments, available days and hours).

### Dashboard and usage controls
Access everything from your dashboard: see how many messages you've used, manage your uploaded documents, view your conversation history, and adjust your assistant's behavior at any time.

### Lead and contact capture
Your assistant can collect customer names, emails, phone numbers, and inquiry details during the conversation — so you always have the contact information you need.

## Getting Started

Getting Evolvian live is straightforward. Here's how it works:

1. **Create your account** at evolvianai.net and access your dashboard.
2. **Choose a plan** — start with Free if you want to try it out first.
3. **Upload your content** — add your business documents (PDF or TXT), FAQs, or any information you want your assistant to know.
4. **Configure your assistant** — set the assistant's name, behavior, and any specific instructions.
5. **Install the chat widget** — copy a short code snippet from your dashboard and paste it into your website. No developer required for most sites.
6. **Connect additional channels** (optional) — add WhatsApp Business or email if needed.
7. **Go live** — your assistant is ready. Monitor conversations from your dashboard history.

Many teams go live in less than one day.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting started

**How do I sign up for Evolvian?**
Go to evolvianai.net and create your account. You can start on the Free plan immediately — no credit card required.

**How long does setup take?**
Most teams are live in under a day. The main steps are uploading your content, configuring your assistant, and adding the widget to your website. All of this is done through your dashboard.

**Do I need a developer to get started?**
No. Most setups don't require technical help. The widget installation is a short code snippet you copy from your dashboard and paste into your website. If you need help, our support team is available at support@evolvianai.com.

**How do I add the chat widget to my website?**
After setting up your assistant, your dashboard gives you a code snippet to paste into your website's HTML. It takes a few minutes. You can also embed the chat in a specific section of your site if you prefer that layout.

**What file types can I upload?**
PDF and TXT files are supported.

**How many documents can I upload?**
It depends on your plan. Free and Starter plans include 1 document. Premium includes 3 documents. White Label has no limit.

**Does Evolvian work in English and Spanish?**
Yes. Evolvian supports both English and Spanish. The assistant automatically detects the language the customer is writing in and responds in the same language.

### Channels

**Which channels does Evolvian support?**
Evolvian works on three channels: your website (via the chat widget), WhatsApp, and email. You can use one or all three, depending on your plan and setup.

**Can I use Evolvian only on my website, without WhatsApp?**
Yes. The website chat widget works independently. You don't need WhatsApp to get started.

**How does WhatsApp integration work?**
You connect your WhatsApp Business account through your Evolvian dashboard. Once connected, your assistant can receive and respond to customer messages on WhatsApp automatically, using your business content and configured instructions. Available on Starter and above.

**Can I use Evolvian for email?**
Yes. Evolvian supports email workflows for appointment confirmations and follow-up communication. This feature is available on Premium and above.

### Appointments & calendar

**Can Evolvian book appointments?**
Yes. Your assistant can handle the full booking flow: it shows available time slots, collects the customer's information, confirms the appointment, and sends reminders. This works on web chat and WhatsApp.

**Does Evolvian send appointment reminders?**
Yes. Evolvian can send automatic reminders before the appointment to help reduce no-shows. Available on eligible plans (Premium and above).

**Does it sync with my Google Calendar?**
Yes. When you connect Google Calendar, Evolvian only shows time slots that are actually free on your calendar, avoiding double-bookings. You control the booking rules: how much advance notice is required, buffer time between appointments, which days are available, and how far ahead customers can book.

**What happens after a booking is confirmed?**
The appointment is recorded and the customer receives a confirmation. If reminders are configured, they are sent automatically before the appointment date.

### Customization

**Can I give the assistant a custom name?**
Yes. You can set your assistant's name from the dashboard. Customers will see this name in the chat interface.

**Can I control how the assistant responds?**
Yes. You can configure a custom prompt — a set of instructions that defines your assistant's tone, behavior, and scope. For example, you can instruct it to always ask for a phone number before booking, or to stay focused only on your services.

**Can I train the assistant on my own documents?**
Yes. Upload your PDFs or TXT files and the assistant will use that content to answer customer questions. The more complete and clear your content is, the better the answers will be.

**Can I see my conversation history?**
Yes. Your dashboard includes a history view where you can review all conversations, monitor assistant performance, and spot areas for improvement.

### Security & privacy

**Is my data secure?**
Yes. Each Evolvian account is isolated — your data is never mixed with another client's. Documents and conversations are stored securely with controlled access.

**Is my data used to train AI models?**
No. Your uploaded documents and conversation data are not shared with third parties for model training purposes.

**Who can access my data?**
Access is controlled and limited to support, troubleshooting, and legal compliance purposes only. Your business content is yours.

**Does Evolvian handle privacy consent for customers?**
Yes. The widget experience includes privacy and legal links. Consent controls are available where applicable.
